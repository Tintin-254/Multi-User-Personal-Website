Before You Continue

Please review how this application handles your data:

🔐
End-to-End Encryption
Your financial data is encrypted locally in your browser using AES-256 encryption. Only you can read your data — other users cannot access it.
💾
Local Storage
All data is stored in your browser's local storage. Nothing is sent to external servers. Clearing your browser data will remove all records.
👤
User Isolation
Each user account has its own encrypted data space. Passwords are hashed and cannot be recovered. The admin can see user accounts but not your financial entries.
📅
Data Retention
Your data is retained until you delete it or clear your browser. You can export your data at any time from the application.
